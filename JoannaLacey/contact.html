I ran my own business selling advanced jewelry designs, beginning in 2009. I also added stones for jewelers to my inventory.I social marketed through Facebook,Etsy,WordPress and other networks. I created professional quality product photos and marketing material through Photoshop.
I also updated inventory and website content through several CMS websites for eCommerce. I opted to close up shop during 2013 to focus on my degree in Web Design and Development, so I could have time to further explore my newfound appreciation for web design and social media marketing.
In 2011, around the time I started selling stones, I founded a group of international jewelry artisans, named Wire Geeks. It grew to a sizable group of artisans with over 1800 members in our chat forum on the cms eCommerce website, Etsy. There are also over 1800 members on my Facebook page for the team.
My responsibilities up to this point for the team have been social marketing, content creation and graphic design. On top of my other responsibilities, I have had the unique opportunity to build healthy business relationships and trust with several top-notch artisans/small business owners from both the US and International countries.
I still continue to market this group today. My plans are to build a social website for them during the next couple of years, this will be my senior project as I earn my degree. I have gained much experience in many areas marketing both my own wares and other artisans through Wire Geeks.
